    """

        factor \= 1.0 \+ 0.35 \* Hprog          *\# Hprog=0.75 → factor≈1.26；Hprog=0.95 → ≈1.33*

    elif mode \== "adaptive":

        factor \= 1.0 \+ 0.4 \* Hprog \- 0.8 \* abs(delta\_norm)  *\# δ\_norm 小 → 更寬鬆*
